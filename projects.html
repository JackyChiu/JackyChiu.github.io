Carleton EngComp 2015

| Oct 2015

Collaborated with team of 4 for programming compeition.

Read more

The challenge was to create a software that could determine if a washer was defected or not by being given the image. The challenge was open to all years. Our team of consisted of 4 first year sotware enginnering students. We programmed our software in Pyhton using the Cimpl (Carleton Image Processing Manipulation Python Libary) created by our prof from PIL (Pyhton Imaging Libary). Our software was 62% accurate of determining faultly washers with the example image set given.
