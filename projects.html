Waker

| Present

A smart alarm designed for iOS.

Read more

The smart alarm is being made with Swift in Xcode for iOS. The idea of my alarm clock is to be a smart alarm clock and include some of the following features: display the amount of time until you have to wake up, notifies you when you should be going to sleep, records your sleep duration and graphs it, notifies you when your amount of sleep has been going down, gradually increase the sound of the alarm at the set time, and have a small task to complete before you are able to turn off the alarm to help you wake up.

Calculator

| Jan 2016

A simple calculator designed for iOS.

Read more

A calculator made with Swift in Xcode for iOS. Done as a project to explore mobile application development. The calculator works with multiple numbers and operations, decimal numbers, and squares. An ANS key was added to recall the last answer. Order of precedence has been implemented. The calculator features a screen that displays what you're calculating as you punch in the inputs and operations. You can also swipe up and down to change the colours of the operation buttons. The functionality of the calculator was inspired by my current Casio fx calculator. Another motivation for creating this is that I did not enjoy the default calculator provided on iOS.

Carleton EngComp 2015

| Oct 2015

Collaborated with team of 4 for programming compeition.

Read more

The challenge was to create a software that could determine if a washer was defected or not by being given the image. The challenge was open to all years. Our team of consisted of 4 first year software engineering students. We programmed our software in Python using the Cimpl (Carleton Image Processing Manipulation Python Libary) created by our prof from PIL (Python Imaging Libary). Our software was 62% accurate of determining faulty washers with the example image set given.
